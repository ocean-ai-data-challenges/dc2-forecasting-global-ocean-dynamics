## Ocean forecasting — key papers

- Lellouche, J.-M. et al. (2021). [The Copernicus Global 1/12° Oceanic and Sea Ice
  GLORYS12 Reanalysis](https://doi.org/10.3389/feart.2021.698876). *Frontiers in Earth Science*, 9.

- Lguensat, R. et al. (2019). [EddyNet: A Deep Neural Network For Pixel-Wise Classification
  of Oceanic Eddies](https://doi.org/10.1109/IGARSS.2019.8900178). *IGARSS 2019*.

- Buongiorno Nardelli, B. et al. (2023). [Deep learning for quality control of surface
  ocean acoustic tomography observations](https://doi.org/10.5194/gmd-16-6127-2023).
  *Geoscientific Model Development*, 16.

- Brajard, J. et al. (2021). [Combining data assimilation and machine learning to infer
  unresolved scale parametrization](https://doi.org/10.1098/rsta.2020.0047).
  *Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society A*, 379.

- Fablet, R. et al. (2023). [Multimodal learning of ocean dynamics for short-term sea
  surface height forecasting](https://doi.org/10.5194/gmd-16-4671-2023).
  *Geoscientific Model Development*, 16.

## Observation instruments

- Verron, J. et al. (2015). [The SARAL/AltiKa Altimetry Satellite Mission]
  (https://doi.org/10.3390/rs7010039). *Remote Sensing*, 7(1).

- Durand, M. et al. (2010). [Estimating Phytoplankton Community Composition from
  Space: SWOT perspectives](https://doi.org/10.5270/OceanObs09.cwp.23). *OceanObs'09*.

- Roemmich, D. et al. (2009). [The Argo Program: Observing the global ocean with
  profiling floats](https://doi.org/10.5670/oceanog.2009.36). *Oceanography*, 22(2).

- Lambin, J. et al. (2010). [The OSTM/Jason-2 Mission]
  (https://doi.org/10.5670/oceanog.2010.41). *Oceanography*, 23(3).
  *(Jason-3 is its direct successor, same orbit geometry.)*

## Lagrangian metrics & ocean transport

- van Sebille, E. et al. (2018). [Lagrangian ocean analysis: Fundamentals and practices]
  (https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ocemod.2017.11.008). *Ocean Modelling*, 121.

## ML for ocean / climate

- Irrgang, C. et al. (2021). [Towards neural Earth system modelling by integrating

  (https://doi.org/10.1038/s42256-021-00374-3). *Nature Machine Intelligence*, 3.

- Bi, K. et al. (2023). [Accurate medium-range global weather forecasting with 3D neural

  relevant for the neural-network forecasting approach context.)*
